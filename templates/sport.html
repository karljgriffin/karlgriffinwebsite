Sporting Achievements

I specialised in the 800 metres from 2009 until 2018. In 2012 at the age of 16, I broke the Irish Youth (u18) 800m record with a time of 1:49.63. In 2014 I went on to run 1:47.44 at the Morton Games in Santry Dublin to qualify for the World Junior (u20) Athletics Championships in Eugene, Oregon. From 2014 until 2018 I was sponsored by New Balance. In 2018 I took the difficult decision to step away from running due to recurring injury issues, deciding to focus on my academic career instead.

Sporting Qualifications

I gained comprehensive knowledge regarding running and exercise physiology over the years through hands-on experience as well as studying the relevant literature. I have also completed several qualifications in these areas. I am currently a qualified athletics coach and a qualified sports masseuse. In the future I would like to complete more courses and upskill myself further. I retain an avid interest in the world of athletics and health / fitness in general. Feel free to reach out to me if you are interested in discussing anything running or fitness-related.
